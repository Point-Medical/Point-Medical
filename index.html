Case Studies

  • Problem

    A leading pediatric rehabilitation center in the GCC region faced challenges in providing customized prosthetic solutions for children with varying growth patterns and unique needs. Standard prosthetics were not adequately addressing the specific requirements of their young patients, leading to discomfort and limited mobility.

    Solution

    POINT developed a tailored line of pediatric prosthetics using advanced materials and innovative design techniques. The solutions were adjustable to accommodate growth, ensuring long-term usability and comfort. POINT also provided ongoing support and adjustments as needed, in collaboration with the rehabilitation center’s medical team.

  • Problem

    Large hospitals in the GCC region are facing inefficiencies in the fitting and customization process of prosthetics for their patients. The traditional methods were time-consuming, often requiring multiple fittings and adjustments, which delayed patient recovery and increased costs.

    Solution

    POINT introduced a digital workflow that included 3D scanning and printing technologies, allowing for precise and rapid customization of prosthetic devices. The process was streamlined from assessment to delivery, reducing the need for multiple physical fittings and allowing for quicker adjustments.

  • Problem

    A growing healthcare provider in the GCC region wanted to differentiate itself in the competitive market by offering state-of-the-art prosthetic solutions that would attract a larger patient base and new investors. However, they lacked the expertise to implement such advanced technologies on their own.

    Solution

    POINT is offering partnership with the healthcare providers to introduce a range of innovative prosthetics, including smart prosthetics equipped with sensor technology for enhanced functionality and user experience. POINT also provide comprehensive training for the provider’s staff, ensuring successful implementation and ongoing support. insurer.
